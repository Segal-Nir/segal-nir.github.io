About

I am a PhD student at the Linguistics Department of the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
Before this, I studied Linguistics at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, where I worked mainly with Prof. Luka Crnič and with Prof. Yosef Grodzinsky at the Neurolinguistics Lab.
My main interest is formal semantics and its interaction with syntax, pragmatics, psycholinguistics, and the philosophy of language.
